Dive to your world

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Dive to your world is an iPad app. Enhancing the user's space experience by redesigning the target bookstore's planning layout, improving the user's reading content and checkout speed in the bookstore with the application on the electronic device, and cooperating with the e-commerce to provide users with the more favorable purchase price and delivery ways to give users a more immersive reading experience.

DESIGN PROCESS

01

Defining Project Scope

In order to achieve the best design results, I decided to select a physical bookstore with a large flow rate and a good geographical location for targeted research and design.

-Investigating the main user group and their activity time.
-Understanding the readers' expectations and requirements for objective conditions such as the location of the bookstore, the surrounding   environment, and the atmosphere of the furnishings.
-Understanding the conditions that physical bookstores are more attractive than e-commerce.
-Designing and optimizing the reader's activity flow in the physical bookstore for their needs.

02

Selecting the Existing Physical  Bookstore

After screening, I finally chose the "TODAY'S READING" bookstore as the design project which is located in Jindi Square, Qujiang District, Xi'an.

Background

The bookstore is surrounded by tourist areas and residential areas. There is no same type of commercial complex in the surrounding area, so the bookstore has influenced a large area.

03

Concept Development

Synthesis Findings: Major Problems and Goals

Based on the feedback of online and offline readers, the four pain points of the existing physical bookstore readers are acquired, and I proposed further consideration and solutions.

Final Concept

The final concept, which is named Dive to Your World, is a system combined conceptual bookstore design with an app of iPad that creates an immersive reading experience to guide readers and users to better enjoy their time at the bookstore.
